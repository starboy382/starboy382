My Personal List Of Back-to-School Items

Hi, the name's Paul and this is my own compilation/lists of things I think are required when returning back to school.

This list will be ordered in a manner of priority, that is; the most important items come first.


  1. VERY IMPORTANT

    This list comprises of items that are required of every student irrespective of whatever course you'll be studying.
    1. A School Bag
    2. Excecise Notes

    3. Pens/Biros
      4. An image of a Pen
    4. A File or Folder Bag

  2. IMPORTANT ITEMS

    Now though this is an "important" list, the items here will vary according to what course the case student is studying.

    The case study student here will be studying the course Computer Science.

    1. A Smartphone
    2. A Laptop
    3. A Modem or a Wi-fi Device
    4. A Flash Drive

      5. A totally unrelated link to a tabular form of the aforementioned Back-to-School lists.

  3. THE OTHER STUFF [ITEMS]

    Do not mistake it, these list of items are also important too but in the aspect of welfare.
    • Pocket Money
    • Provisions
    • Association/Departmental or Faculty fees
